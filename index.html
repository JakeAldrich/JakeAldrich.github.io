About

Hello! My name is Jake Aldrich, and I am a recent college graduate of Central Michigan University. I achieved my bachelors in information technology, with a focus in web development, and multimedia design. Embracing my competitive side, I was successful in both my grades, as well as my hobbies.


A driving passion that pushed me down this path was the rush of seeing an idea come to life. Working on interactive web sites, and utilizing tools such as the Adobe Suite, and Unity has helped me persue this.

While working on my degree, I was employed as a systems analyst at the CMU Office of Information Technology Help Desk. During this time I supported an audience of over 30,000 people through phone, chat, and in-person support. Contributing more than just daily activities, I created new knowledgebase articles documenting information for future employees.
